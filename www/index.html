About

patchlogue is a tool to help you visualize the patches you created on your KORG Prologue synthesizer.

Just plug your KORG Prologue in, select the appropriate MIDI INs and OUTs, and start visualizing your patches !

In one glance, you can inspect and get a sense of the parameters used to make up the sound of your patches. You can also save your patches so that you can visualize them later.

patchlogue addresses the issue that plagues synthesizers with patch recall : On loading a saved patch, physical knobs of your synth no longer matches the actual values of the parameters they control.
patchlogue comes in handy in those situations, providing you with a clear visualization of your parameters.

For any bugs / feature requests, head over to the Github repository.

Let's go!

patchlogue gives you a beautiful visualization of your patches created on the KORG Prologue 8 / KORG Prologue 16.

  1. Plug in your synthesizer using a USB cable
  2. Give `patchlogue` the permission for MIDI access
  3. First, select the MIDI input in the MIDI input dropdown. If you didn't modify your factory settings, it should be : PROLOGUE KBD/KNB
  4. Second, select the MIDI output in the MIDI output dropdown. If you didn't modify your factory settings, it should be : PROLOGUE SND
  5. Hit the `retrieve patch` button and you can now see all your parameters conveniently laid out üéâ!

Supported synthesizers

  • KORG Prologue 8
  • KORG Prologue 16
